How To Make Rice

You should follow these steps in order. Duh :)

  1. Get a pot and a lid
  2. Put 1 cup of rice in the pot
  3. Put 2 cups of water in the pot
    • If you use hot water from the faucet, it will boil faster.
  4. Heat on high until boiling
  5. Cover and set to low heat
  6. Set n 8 minute timer
  7. Wait...
  8. Turn off heat
    • I usually leave it on the burner.
  9. Set a 15 minute timer
  10. Enjoy!

DON'T OPEN THE LID DURING THE COOKING PROCESS
